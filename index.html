Intentions of Vote in Chile Dictatorship Plebiscite 1989

Intentions of Vote - Chile Dictatorship Plebiscite 1989

National survey conducted in April and May of 1988 by FLACSO/Chile.

FLACSO (Facultad Latinoamericana de Ciencias Sociales/Latinamerican Faculty of Social Sciences) is a UNESCO initiative created in 1957.

This graph represents visually the Voting intentions in the Plebiscite that would determine if Chile would continue under Dictatorship ('Yes') or return to democracy ('No'). Other options are A for"Abstain" and U for "Undecided".

It is important to remember that people contrary to the regime did not feel at liberty of expressing political opinion
